Emergency Medical Situations

Know what to do in critical situations

Heart Attack

Call emergency services immediately if experiencing severe chest pain.

Stroke

Seek medical help immediately if there is slurred speech or facial drooping.

Choking

Perform the Heimlich maneuver if someone is choking and cannot breathe.

Burns

Cool the burn under running water for 10-15 minutes and cover with a sterile dressing.

Allergic Reaction

Administer epinephrine (if available) and seek emergency help.

Asthma Attack

Use an inhaler and seek medical help if breathing does not improve.

Seizure

Keep the person safe, avoid restraining them, and call emergency services.

Spinal Injury

Do not move the person; keep them still and call emergency services.

Heat Stroke

Move the person to a cool place, hydrate them, and seek medical attention.
